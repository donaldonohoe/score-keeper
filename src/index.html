About App

Score Keeper keeps track of player scores and performance in any multi-player game. See realtime player rankings and stats with Score Keeper's visual leaderboard UI. Perfect for games like Scrabble, Score Keeper can be used on mobile or desktop, and will always remember your game with session storage.

Score Keeper is designed and developed by Donal Donohoe. See more at donaldonohoe.io.
